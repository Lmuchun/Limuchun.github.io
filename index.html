About Me

I am Muchun Li, a postgraduate in the Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping, and Remote Sensing at Wuhan University, China. I was working under the supervision of Dr. Wei Wang. My research focuses on understanding and modeling land-use dynamics to support urban sustainability. I apply several models for land-use change simulation, especially for hybrid approaches incorporating cellular automata and artificial intelligence algorithms.

On this website, I present some of my scientific research and write about things that I find interesting, mostly related to my research life.
